Placement Strategy for Final Year Students

Targets:
- 3 primary roles
- 20 quality applications per week
- 2 mock interviews per week

Weekly Process:
Monday:
- Role-specific resume updates

Networking Block (2 times per week):
- Send focused LinkedIn outreach to alumni and product teams.
- Ask for referral opportunities after sharing portfolio and metric-driven project summaries.
- Track outreach response rate and follow up politely in 5-7 days.

Tuesday:
- Application submissions with tailored cover notes

Wednesday:
- Coding and SQL practice

Thursday:
- Project improvement and README updates

Friday:
- Behavioral interview preparation

Saturday:
- Mock interview and feedback review

Sunday:
- Plan next week and track progress

Tracking Metrics:
- Applications sent
- Response rate
- Interview conversion
- Offer conversion

Supply Chain Analyst Career Path

Core Skills:
- Demand forecasting and planning fundamentals
- Inventory optimization and safety stock reasoning
- Logistics and distribution performance analysis
- Supplier performance tracking and procurement analytics
- KPI dashboards for service level, lead time, and fill rate

Learning Plan:
Phase 1:
- Understand end-to-end supply chain flows and planning cycles.

Phase 2:
- Analyze demand variability and forecast error patterns.

Phase 3:
- Build inventory and replenishment scenarios with trade-off analysis.

Phase 4:
- Present operational recommendations with cost and service-level impact.

Interview Focus:
- Handling stock-outs and overstock risk
- Forecast accuracy improvement techniques
- Lead-time reduction strategy and supplier collaboration
- KPI-driven decision-making under constraints

Question Bank:
- How do you improve forecast accuracy for seasonal products?
- How would you balance service level and inventory carrying cost?
- What metrics do you use to evaluate supplier performance?
- How do you diagnose recurring logistics delays?

Portfolio Signals:
- Inventory optimization case with scenario comparisons
- Forecast error analysis dashboard
- Supply chain KPI scorecard with recommendations

Project Manager Path for Early Career Professionals

Core Skills:
- Scope definition and work breakdown structures
- Sprint planning and milestone tracking
- Risk identification, mitigation planning, and escalation
- Cross-functional communication and stakeholder alignment
- Delivery metrics and post-project retrospectives

Learning Plan:
Month 1:
- Learn project lifecycle, delivery models, and communication plans.

Month 2:
- Practice sprint planning, backlog grooming, and status reporting.

Month 3:
- Run a simulated project with risk register and timeline management.

Portfolio Projects:
- Academic project delivery plan with dependency mapping
- Risk mitigation plan for multi-team software rollout
- Stakeholder communication template with milestone dashboards

Interview Focus:
- Handling scope creep and timeline pressure
- Prioritization under resource constraints
- Risk management trade-offs
- Team conflict resolution and decision-making

Readiness Signals:
- Consistent milestone tracking with measurable outcomes
- Clear communication artifacts and meeting summaries
- Evidence of delivery improvements after retrospectives

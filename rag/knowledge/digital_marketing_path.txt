Digital Marketing Career Path

Core Skills:
- Search engine optimization (SEO) fundamentals
- Performance marketing across Google Ads and social platforms
- Conversion funnel analysis and campaign measurement
- Content strategy and audience segmentation
- Web analytics using GA4 and UTM tracking

Learning Plan:
Phase 1:
- Learn channel fundamentals: search, social, email, and content.

Phase 2:
- Build campaign plans with budget, targeting, and expected ROI.

Phase 3:
- Analyze campaign performance using CTR, CPC, CAC, and conversion rate.

Phase 4:
- Present optimization recommendations with clear business impact.

Interview Focus:
- SEO audit approach and prioritization
- Performance marketing budget allocation decisions
- Attribution model trade-offs
- A/B testing for landing pages and ad creatives

Question Bank:
- How do you improve low conversion rate campaigns?
- How would you measure campaign ROI with limited tracking?
- What is your process for keyword research and intent mapping?
- How do you decide between brand and performance budgets?

Portfolio Signals:
- Campaign optimization case study with before-after metrics
- Funnel improvement analysis with recommendation rationale
- Dashboard summarizing channel-wise ROI and CAC trends

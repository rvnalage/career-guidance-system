Career Decision Framework

Inputs:
- Current skills
- Interests
- Education level
- Time available
- Preferred work type (research, product, engineering, analytics)

Scoring Method:
1) Skills fit score
2) Interest fit score
3) Education fit score
4) Market demand score

Decision Rule:
Choose top 2 paths by total score, then run a 4-week experiment for each path.

4-Week Path Experiment:
Week 1:
Read role expectations and required tools.

Week 2:
Complete one guided project.

Week 3:
Create one mini independent project.

Week 4:
Do mock interview and self-review.

Evaluation Questions:
- Did I enjoy daily tasks?
- Could I learn quickly?
- Was I able to produce portfolio evidence?
- Is there hiring demand in this role?

Output:
Pick one primary path and one backup path with a 90-day action plan.

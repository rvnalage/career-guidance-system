Interview Preparation for MTech Students

Goal:
Prepare for technical and behavioral interviews in 6 weeks.

Week 1:
Review core CS topics: data structures, algorithms, DBMS basics, OS basics.
Solve 3 easy and 2 medium coding problems daily.

Week 2:
Study SQL deeply: joins, subqueries, window functions, indexing.
Practice 20 SQL interview questions with explanations.

Week 3:
Revise machine learning basics: bias-variance, overfitting, train-validation-test split, evaluation metrics.
Prepare short explanations for common models: linear regression, logistic regression, decision tree, random forest.

Week 4:
Project storytelling practice:
- Problem statement
- Data source and quality
- Approach and model choice
- Metrics and business impact
- Limitations and next steps

Week 5:
Behavioral interview prep using STAR method.
Prepare answers for:
- Tell me about yourself
- Conflict in a team
- Failure and learning
- Why this role

Week 6:
Mock interviews:
- 2 coding mocks
- 2 ML/system discussion mocks
- 1 HR mock

Checklist:
- Resume aligned with role
- GitHub and portfolio links working
- 2-minute self-introduction ready
- Questions to ask interviewer prepared

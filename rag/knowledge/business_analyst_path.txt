Business Analyst Path for MTech Students

Core Skills:
- Requirement gathering and clarification
- Business process mapping and gap analysis
- User stories and acceptance criteria writing
- SQL fundamentals for reporting and validation
- Stakeholder communication and meeting facilitation

Learning Plan:
Step 1:
- Learn business analysis lifecycle and requirement types.

Step 2:
- Practice process mapping using as-is and to-be workflows.

Step 3:
- Write user stories with clear acceptance criteria for sample products.

Step 4:
- Build dashboard summaries and communicate recommendations to stakeholders.

Portfolio Projects:
- Loan approval workflow optimization case study
- Requirement specification document for campus placement portal
- Product backlog and user story map for student service app

Interview Focus:
- Requirement gathering scenarios
- Conflict resolution across stakeholders
- Prioritization using business impact and effort
- Functional versus non-functional requirement clarity

Networking Strategy:
- Connect with business analysts and product teams on LinkedIn.
- Request informational calls with alumni in consulting and operations roles.
- Share one-page case summaries to improve referral conversations.

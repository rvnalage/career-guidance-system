Product Analyst Career Path

Networking Keywords: linkedin, alumni, referral, outreach

Core Skills:
- SQL and dashboards
- Product metrics design
- Experimentation and A/B testing
- Funnel and cohort analysis
- Stakeholder communication

Learning Plan:
Step 1:
- Understand product lifecycle and user journey

Step 2:
- Define metrics: activation, retention, conversion

Step 3:
- Build funnel and cohort analyses

Step 4:
- Run experiment analysis and recommendations

Portfolio Projects:
- Conversion funnel diagnosis
- Retention improvement analysis
- Feature impact evaluation report

Interview Focus:
- Metric selection rationale
- Causality vs correlation
- Experiment pitfalls
- Business recommendation quality

Networking Strategy:
- Build a focused LinkedIn presence around product metrics, experimentation, and SQL case studies.
- Reach out to alumni working as product analysts and request short informational calls.
- Ask for referral opportunities after sharing a concise portfolio and impact summary.
- Plan weekly outreach goals and track response quality.

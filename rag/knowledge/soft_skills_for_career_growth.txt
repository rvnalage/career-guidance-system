Soft Skills for Career Growth

Priority Skills:
- Structured communication
- Problem decomposition
- Time management
- Team collaboration
- Feedback handling

Practice Routine:
Daily:
- Write one concise status update

Weekly:
- Present one technical topic in 5 minutes

Bi-weekly:
- Peer feedback session on communication

Monthly:
- Reflect on one conflict and resolution strategy

Career Impact:
Strong soft skills increase interview performance, team trust, and leadership opportunities.

Higher Studies vs Job Decision Guide

Consider Higher Studies If:
- You want specialization and research depth
- Target roles require advanced credentials
- You have clear area of interest and funding plan

Consider Job First If:
- You need practical exposure and income stability
- You want to test domain fit in industry
- You can build profile and return for masters later

Decision Criteria:
- Career goal clarity
- Financial constraints
- Opportunity cost
- Learning preference
- Timeline flexibility

Action Plan:
- Compare 2-year outcomes for both paths
- Talk to 3 seniors from each path
- Decide based on evidence, not peer pressure
